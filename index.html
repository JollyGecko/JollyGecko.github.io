Professional Growth

I started my path in software development in the insurance & banking sector. This experience was transformative, as I learned to work with real end clients, manage deadlines, and collaborate with diverse teams. The fast-paced environment pushed me to grow quickly and adapt to new technologies.

From there, I went into the automotive industry, working on features for autonomous driving. The research-driven approach has presented a very interesting challenge, along with the criticality of the product, leading to a better understanding of safety practices.
